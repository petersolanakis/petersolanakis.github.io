Our Story

Nick Solanakis has been successfully operating N.G Building Cleaning since 1989. Working in the industry from as young as 15 years old with his father, Nick steadily gained experience managing his own contracts. With over 10 years of experience and his father soon to retire, Nick decided to start his own venture.

It wasn't long before he won contracts for A&P Canada (now known as Metro) and Food Basics stores across Ottawa, Brockville, and Arnprior, serving these contracts for over 8 years.

Now, N.G Building Cleaning proudly cleans for more than 30 clients across the Ottawa region. Nick now happily leads his three sons who clean with him, continuing the family tradition that he grew up in.

What makes us unique? We're a family run business with a huge amount of pride in the work we do. We promise excellent quality in the work we deliver, and responsive & personable customer service that adapts to your needs and concerns.
